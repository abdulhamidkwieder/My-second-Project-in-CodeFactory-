Get started

1. Registration

Regitration to Jugendcollege via FSW/MA17 or AMS Wien Zubuchung zum Jugendcollege über

  • FSW/MA17 -> Bildungsdrehscheibe -> jugendcollege
  • AMS -> Infotalk -> jugendcollege

Basic Census of educational need and criteria check (Age, Residence,..)

2. Entry phase

  • Clearing
  • Consultation
  • Admission to jugendcollege
    • Creation of an individual education plan
    • Scheduling of modules for the first learning phase (duration: 8 weeks) depending on the students level of German, professional skills and education goals

3. Participation of Educational programm

  • Core modules (German modules)
    • Level 1: Basic 1 and 2
    • Level 2: Basic 3, 4, and 5; German as a second language A1 and A2
    • Level 3: German as a second language B1; Langauge, Job, Education
  • Special modules (Mandatory and electiv modules)
    • These will be combined depending on level, prior knowledege and educational or professional goals.
      For example: Critical participation, literacy in first language, competence fields of PSA, Peer-Translation
  • Module-based offers
    • Socially integrative activities
    • Educational and professional counseling (Reflection of individual learning routes and division of learning groups for the next phase or preperation for the transfer
    • Socio-pedagogical support
    • Supportive programs: Learning aid from volunteers, socio-pedagogical support, internal and external counselling institutions

4. Transfer and follow-up support

  • Placement in another secondary school, professional training or job market
    • Before the transfer: Preperation with the participant; trial days in schools or at the training organisation; communication with the future contact person
    • After the transfer: Follow-up support at jugendcollege if needed
